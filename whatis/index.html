home papers teaching my github what is...

The AMS What is... collection presents snapshots of various areas of math, and I've enjoyed reading them and learning (very roughly) what other mathematicians work on. On the other hand, I've often found it difficult to answer when my students ask "so what do you work on?" and this is my attempt to fix that. So, what is...

  • ...a Polytope?
  • ...a Matroid?
  • ...a Semigraphoid?
